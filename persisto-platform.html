Persisto Decision Architecture

A unified decision system implemented as an underwriting decision layer.

Why Decision Quality Degrades at Scale

Fragmented Decision Inputs

Submission quality varies across brokers, formats, and sources, reducing comparability and consistency at the start of each decision.

Inconsistent Expression of Risk Appetite

Centrally defined risk intent is applied unevenly across teams and regions, introducing variance into daily underwriting decisions.

Delayed Visibility into Decision Quality

Decision quality becomes observable only after portfolio performance has already shifted.

Persisto - Unified Decision System

A product suite designed to bring structure and control to underwriting decisions as organizations scale.

Mark — Submission Engine

Consistent Decision Starting Points

Structures fragmented broker submissions into a clean, review-ready case file, reducing early decision variance and rework.

Sia — External Data Engine

Complete Risk Context at Submission

Enriches broker-provided information with property, business, inspection, and public-record data, eliminating manual research and blind spots.

ARGEN — Risk & Governance Engine

Embedded Risk Governance

Applies underwriting logic consistently at scale, flags anomalies, and produces an explainable risk profile suitable for audit and review.

APEX - Algorithmic Pricing and Execution Engine

Dynamic Rater Ingestion

Transforms Excel rater sheets into high-performance APIs for Straight Through Quoting (STQ).

Sandy — Underwriter Assistant

Lower Cognitive Load at Decision Time

Answers questions, summarizes files, compares submissions, and drafts notes so underwriters spend less time searching and more time deciding.

Amy — Phone Automation

Uninterrupted Underwriting Focus

Handles routine broker calls, captures missing information, and routes escalations without pulling underwriters out of decision flow.

Ava — Client Risk Coach

Actionable Risk Guidance for Insurers

Translates underwriting insights into clear recommendations that help insureds reduce risk and support stronger renewals and pricing discussions.

Vera - Verification Engine

Decision Reconstruction

Provides an immutable audit trail for every quote, ensuring 100% regulatory compliance and transparency.
